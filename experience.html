Nanomanufacturing Lab

Undergraduate Researcher

February 2016-present

I am an undergraduate researcher in Dr. Hayden Taylor's Design for Nanomanufacturing Lab at UC Berkeley, where I collect data for the lab's Nanocalipers project. The project aims to build a system that determines the mechanical properties of thin films with high resolution. As one of the undergraduate researchers in the lab, I was trained on the optical system and am tasked with operating lab equipment including a spin coater and a UV aligner to prepare samples and collect video data.
Wind Tunnel Lab

Lab Assistantana

Summer 2016

I was part of a team of undergraduate students in Dr. John Larue's Wind Tunnel Lab at UC Irvine, where I calibrated and operated an open circuit wind tunnel to collect experiment data and manufactured hot and cold wire anemometers.
