Chinatown

On the way to the bookstore
we discovered Chinatown.

Shops
to the left
                         and right
     of us--
small, bright, quaint,
red and yellow and bronze

mini-bazaars.

Turn left,
                         here is a park.
Turn right,
                         here is a church.

The lions took us here.
They took us from where we were
     and where we wanted to go

and left us in a street
of spices
and tourist traps
with scarlet paper lanterns
dangling above
like loose teeth.

We follow the crowds
     and the hills
          and the stores.

They are all the same.
Cloth owls, knit hats, silk scarves,
novelty gum, and San Francisco magnets.
It repeats, repeats, repeats...

An endless array of mini-bazaars
     on our way to

               nowhere.
back.
