insomnia

i time travel at night

my thoughts drift
from moment to moment
leaving me behind

i remember things
i've forgotten
and will forget again
when the sun comes
to burn everything

i remember things
that never happened
and things that won't ever be

i am the past and the future
but never the present

my eyes are open
under closed eyelids--
all i see is yesterday and how good it was
and how bad it was
so so bad

i finally bury my consciousness
in memories of you
and pointless plans
of what i could have done so that you weren't just that,
a memory.
back.
