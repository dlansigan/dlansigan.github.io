Young's Modulus

Flipping papers,
clicking pens,
tapping keyboard keys--
this is the sound of me
     breaking down.

I cannot be torn away
but it tears at me,
it draws me in--
the future draws me in.

Weeks from now is tomorrow
and tomorrow
was yesterday.

Ticking clocks,
whirring fan blades,
cracking knuckles--
this is the sound of me
     being sound,

for I'd rather be
     breaking down
than breaking apart.
