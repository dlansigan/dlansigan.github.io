Hello! My name is Dana. I am a second-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering, and I hope to enter a career in aerospace.

Growing up, I loved making things. Legos, cardboard boxes, used toilet paper rolls—whatever I could get my hands on, I'd transform them into something else. Things I couldn't build I'd draw or write about. I still do creative writing now: in my free time, I enjoy writing short stories and poems as well as songs to play on my ukulele and guitar.

Then when I was about 12, I discovered that by typing combinations of characters into the Neopets profile editor, I could make my profile look a certain way. My parents, who are both programmers, later notified me that this was called HTML. I loved this new thing called programming I had discovered and I later picked up more languages.

On my way to my Mechanical Engineering degree, I'm still building, and I'm excited to continue creating!
