Hello! My name is Dana. I am a second-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering, and I hope to enter a career in aerospace.

Making things is my passion. Be it poems, websites, songs, or RC planes, I love to create. Whenever I learn something new, I want to get my hands dirty right away and find out ways to apply what I've learned. I learn to do and do to learn, and this is why I want to be an engineer.

My dream is to help make something that will fly and maybe even leave the Earth. To me, flight is the most amazing feat of engineering, and I want to be part of that. I mean, how cool would it be to tell people "I helped make that hundred-ton lump of metal fly" or "I helped make dangerous chemicals explode under that thing to throw it off the face of the Earth at 18,000 miles per hour"? Hopefully, one day, I will be able to!
